Here are game fundamentals:

  1. Click with your left mouse button on the hidden square
  2. You can uncover 3 types of fields:
    • - The field doesn't have any bomb nearby,
    • - The field has as many bombs on the fields around it, as the number tells (e.g. this field has five bombs on the fields around it),
    • - You were unlucky to find the bomb, the game is over. You can try again.
  3. Your task is to uncover all of the fields that are not bombs
  4. Click with your right mouse button to place a flag. It will help you to remember where you think the bombs are.
  5. You get points based on the completion time (you don't get any points if you uncover the bomb)
  6. On harder levels the board is bigger and there are more bombs, but you get more points too
