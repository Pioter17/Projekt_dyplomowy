Here are game fundamentals:

  1. Click on the image you want to uncover
  2. Click on the second image and, if they are not the same, try to remember their content and position, as they will cover back in a moment
  3. If the pictures were the same, they will disappear and you don't have to think of them anymore
  4. You get points for clearing the board
  5. The fewer turns and time you spend, the more points you'll get
